Requirements

What We Require From Investors

To keep every branch consistent with the Noblesse standard, we ask each investing partner to meet the following.

Valid Legal Identity

A valid KTP (or passport for foreign investors) and NPWP are required before any cooperation agreement is signed.

Minimum Capital Commitment

Funds must meet the minimum threshold of the chosen tier and be transferred through a notarized escrow arrangement.

Agreement To Brand SOP

The new branch must follow Noblesse's operational, service, and visual branding standards without modification.

Approved Location Survey

The proposed ruko location must pass our internal market and foot-traffic survey before funds are utilized.

Minimum Cooperation Period

A signed commitment of at least 3 years per branch, outlined clearly in the notarized cooperation agreement.

No Conflicting Ownership

Investors may not simultaneously hold ownership in a directly competing barbershop brand within the same city.
